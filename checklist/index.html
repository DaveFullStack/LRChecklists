Little Rituals Checklists

Open Checklist (Barista)

Task
Turn Machine on and all plugs behind the bar (boiler, grinder etc.)

Switch Underneath, if screen shows power off then hold power button for 4 seconds

Wait for machine to reach temperature before doing anything else
Place filters in dishwasher and turn on

Leave until boiler and tank lights turn off
Make the Batch Brew
Recipe on whiteboard
Pull a shot on both groupheads on machine when at correct temperature

Leave in for portafilters and baskets to heat up
Filling the Ice machine

Make sure plug is in the bottom!

Fill with water and press the power button on the ice machine
Dial in house Spro

Recipe on the board. Adjust grind until spro pulling within correct time.

Check yield of spro on scales

Taste and make sure its nice!
Whilst you are dialling in the coffee, you can start on these tasks inbetween pulling shots...
Start putting pastries and cakes out in display

Check the bertinet order!! (Let Toby or Isabelle know if it's wrong)
Top up the soft drink fridge if needed
Bring the flowers up from downstairs
Check stock levels

TA Station - lids, cups, sugars, napkins etc.
Oat Milk
Stamp Cards
Bring up the batch brew from downstairs
Give it a taste and make sure its good

You're ready to open! Have a Legendary Day

Closing Checklist (Coffee Machine)

Task
Get closed basket portafilter and add pulycaff to basket

Place portafilter in grouphead you want to clean!
Hold cleaning cycle button on machine (far right) for a few seconds.

Grouphead right button will start flashing, to start first cycle press the flashing button
Whilst the grouphead is cleaning, take the pyrex bowl from the cleaning cupboard underneath the sink.

Fill the pyrex bowl with boiling water and pulycaff and soak the baskets and portafilters in the water

Make sure the handles of the portafilters are not in the water!
Once first cycle has completed, rinse out the pulycaff from the portafilter and start the second cycle with just the closed basket.
Repeat process for second grouphead
Once the grouphead has ran through both cycles of cleaning, unscrew the screen

Place the screws somehwere safe and DONT LOSE THEM PLEASEEEEE

Place the screen in the pyrex bowl to soak in pulycaff with the portafilter and basket
Use the brush or a cloth to clean inside the grouphead

Make sure to get right up in there and remove all the coffee grounds you can
Remove the drip tray from the machine and clean all the excess coffee grounds and the tray itself
Using a wet cloth and blue roll, go over the machine and make it look nice and shiny and brand new!
Once the machine is looking good, take the portafilters, baskets and screens from the pyrex bowl and give them a rinse in the sink.

Clean any coffee stains that are left on the equipment.
Screw the screens back into the groupheads.

Place the baskets back into the portafilters and put them in the machine!
Turn the machine off using the switch underneath the machine
Clean up the bar area and make it look nice and what not!

Place all the scales on charge please!
You have cleaned the machine and coffee area whooooooooop

General Bar Area Close

Task
Put the coffee gear through the dishwasher
  • Milk Pitchers
  • Pitch Rinder Tray
  • EK Tray
  • Knockbox Lid
  • Cake/Pastry Utensils
  • Ice Tray and Scoop
  • Bar Measures for decaf etc.
  • Puq Press Tray
Clean the pastry/cake display and sandwich display boxes

Place any leftover pastries in a tupperware and date label
Same with the cakes. Some of the cakes will already have boxes, for example the hummingbird Cake
Make sure cakes go in the fridge

The sandwiches cannot be sold the next day. If there are any leftover then put them in the fridge for staff the next day.

Once everything is put away, spray the displays with sanitiser and clean with blue roll
Drain the Ice machine in the bar sink and get rid of any water left in the carafes
Stocking up the bar!
  • TA station - Lids, sugars, napkins etc.
  • TA cups behind the puq press
  • Count the milk and let Isabelle or Toby know if we need to order more
  • Oat Milk
  • Soft Drink Fridge
  • Decaf/Aero/Guest dosing
  • Fill grinder with house beans - Make sure to check the date!
  • Tea bags
  • Spare cups, lids, napkins, blue roll etc. under the bar area
Put all the dirty/wet cloths and tea towels into the laundry bag
Bins!

Empty the coffee bin into the waste bin.
Empty flower station box under sink into waste bin

Take the bins dowstairs or outside if it is bin day

Put new bin bags in

The bar area should be looking pretty good now! nice workkkkk!

Kitchen Close

Task
Do all the washing up!

If there are a couple leftover cups etc. from late customers then please hand wash them and place in dishwasher trays ready for the morning
Drain the dishwasher and take out the filters

To drain the dishwasher you take out the filter in the bottom right, turn the power off and press start
Once the dishwasher has been drained, take out the remaining filters, wipe the inside and get rid of any food etc. on the floor of the dishwasher

Clean off the filters in the sink and place them back inside the machine ready for the next day
Wipe down all the surfaces with sanitiser sprays and clean out the sink
Put all the used cloths/tea towels in the dirty laundry bag
Empty the bins and put new bin bags in!

If the bins are barely full then you can leave them in for the next day

The kitchen is doneeeeeeee oh yeah!
